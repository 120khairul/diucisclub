About CIS Club


Daffodil International University

Welcome to the official digital hub of the Computing and Information Systems Club (CIS Club) at Daffodil International University (DIU). We are the premier student-run organization dedicated to empowering the next generation of computing professionals, innovators, and leaders.


Our Mission & Vision

Our core mission is to foster a dynamic ecosystem of innovation, learning, and collaboration centered on Computing and Information Systems, with a specialized focus on Artificial Intelligence. We bridge the gap between academic theory and real-world application, preparing our members for the forefront of the tech industry. Our vision is to be the catalyst for technological excellence at DIU, creating a community where curiosity is nurtured, ideas are transformed into impactful projects, and students are equipped to solve tomorrow's challenges.


What We Do

Technical Workshops & Hands-on Sessions: We regularly host workshops on trending technologies—from machine learning and data science with Python and R, to web development, cybersecurity, cloud computing, and IoT. These sessions are designed to build practical, industry-relevant skills. AI Innovation Lab: As AI is our strategic focus, we run specialized study groups, project bootcamps, and hackathons focused on AI, machine learning, and deep learning, encouraging members to build intelligent solutions. Expert Talks & Seminars: We connect our members with industry professionals, DIU alumni working in global tech firms, and faculty experts for insightful talks on career paths, emerging trends, and research opportunities. Project-Based Collaboration: We believe in learning by doing. Club members collaborate on diverse projects, building portfolios that showcase their skills in software development, AI model creation, and system design. National Competitions & Hackathons: We organize and participate in inter-university and national-level programming contests, hackathons, and idea competitions, proudly representing DIU and achieving notable accolades. Community Building: We create a supportive network where students from all batches and specializations within computing can connect, share knowledge, and grow together.


Our Home: Daffodil International University

Operating within Daffodil International University (DIU), a leading private university in Bangladesh renowned for its focus on innovation and technology, provides us with immense strength. DIU’s state-of-the-art labs, supportive faculty, and industry-connected curriculum perfectly complement our club's objectives. We work in close collaboration with the Department of Computing and Information System and other relevant faculties to align our activities with academic excellence.


Join Our Community

Whether you are a beginner eager to write your first line of code, an aspiring AI engineer, or a developer looking to collaborate on ambitious projects, the CIS Club is your home. Here, you will find the resources, mentorship, and peer support to excel. Explore our site to discover upcoming events, meet our executive committee, view our project gallery, and learn how you can become part of our story. Let's innovate, code, and build the future, together.
